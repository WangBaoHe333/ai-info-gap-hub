## Style Prompt

Premium, editorial tech promo for a Chinese AI tutorial directory. The video should feel like a calm but sharp product trailer: clean information surfaces, restrained motion, confident Chinese copy, and a mobile-first creator audience. It should look useful rather than gimmicky.

## Colors

- Deep ink foreground: #14211f
- Teal brand accent: #06776f
- Soft mint surface: #eef7f5
- Warm risk accent: #c85f49
- Paper surface: #fbfdfc
- Muted text: #5e6d69

## Typography

- Display: Fraunces, PingFang SC, Microsoft YaHei, serif
- Body/UI: PingFang SC, Microsoft YaHei, system-ui, sans-serif
- Data labels: IBM Plex Mono, SFMono-Regular, monospace

## What NOT to Do

- Do not show admin/backend screens.
- Do not use neon cyberpunk styling or purple-blue dominance.
- Do not imply guaranteed bypassing restrictions; include compliance/risk wording.
- Do not make dense unreadable screenshots; use simplified product mockups.
